Our Story

Vinay Gupta & Associates was born from a simple belief: every taxpayer in India deserves honest, accessible, and expert financial guidance. Founded by Vinay Gupta in Dehradun, the firm started as a small tax practice with a handful of clients and a deep commitment to doing things right. From the very beginning, the focus was on building trust through transparency, not through complicated jargon or hidden charges.

Over the years, what began as a one-person operation grew steadily into a trusted name across Uttarakhand and beyond. Today, we proudly serve over 10,000 clients, from salaried professionals and freelancers to growing businesses and established enterprises. Our growth has never come at the cost of personal attention. Every client, whether filing their first ITR or managing complex GST compliance, receives the same level of care and dedication.

What truly sets us apart is our WhatsApp-first approach. We realized early on that most of our clients prefer quick, informal communication over formal office visits. So we made it simple. Send us a message, share your documents, and let our experts handle the rest. No portals to navigate, no long waiting times. Just real people providing real solutions, right from your phone.
