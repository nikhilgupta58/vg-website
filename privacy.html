Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

At Vinay Gupta & Associates, we collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out the contact form on our website
  • Reach out to us via WhatsApp, phone, or email
  • Engage our services for taxation, GST, or other advisory

This may include your name, phone number, email address, business details, PAN, GST number, and financial documents necessary for providing our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide taxation and advisory services you have requested
  • Communicate with you regarding your filings, returns, and compliance
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal and financial information. All sensitive documents and data are handled with strict confidentiality in accordance with professional standards and applicable laws.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only:

  • With government authorities as required for tax filings and compliance (e.g., Income Tax Department, GST portal)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Tax-related documents are retained as per the requirements of the Income Tax Act and GST laws.

6. Cookies

Our website does not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect personal information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal retention requirements
