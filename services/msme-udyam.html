Udyam / MSME Registration

Register your Micro, Small or Medium enterprise on the free Udyam portal and unlock priority-sector lending, the 45-day MSMED payment rule and tender preference.

What it is

An Aadhaar-linked classification on the free Udyam portal.

Udyam Registration is the official recognition of a business as a Micro, Small or Medium Enterprise under the MSMED Act, 2006. The classification was revised in 2020 and is now based on a combination of investment in plant, machinery and equipment and annual turnover. Manufacturing and service enterprises follow the same composite criterion.

The registration is permanent – there is no renewal. It is filed on the government Udyam portal, is entirely paperless, and is issued free of cost. The Udyam certificate carries a unique Udyam Registration Number (URN) and a dynamic QR code linking to the enterprise details, which lenders, GeM, PSUs and buyers verify directly.

Micro

Investment up to ₹1 crore and turnover up to ₹5 crore.

Small

Investment up to ₹10 crore and turnover up to ₹50 crore.

Medium

Investment up to ₹50 crore and turnover up to ₹250 crore.

Frequently asked

Udyam questions we answer every week.

I have the old Udyog Aadhaar Memorandum – do I need to re-register?

add

Yes. All UAMs had to migrate to Udyam. If you have not migrated, your old Udyog Aadhaar is no longer valid for claiming MSME benefits and you should register fresh on the Udyam portal. We help with migration and data cleanup so the new certificate reflects the correct investment, turnover and NIC code.

Can I register multiple enterprises under one Aadhaar?

add

One PAN and one Udyam per enterprise is the rule. A single Aadhaar holder can have multiple Udyam registrations only if the enterprises are distinct legal entities (different PANs) – for example a proprietorship, an LLP and a company owned by the same person. All branches, divisions or units of the same entity are added as additional units within one Udyam registration, not as separate ones.

Is the registration free? Are the agents asking for a fee legitimate?

add

Udyam Registration on the government portal is entirely free of cost. The Ministry of MSME publishes a list of official Udyam Registration Centres. Third-party websites charging a registration fee in the name of Udyam are not authorised. We charge a professional fee for classification, form filling and advisory – never the registration fee itself.

What happens if my turnover grows above the category limit?

add

Classification is dynamic – the portal auto-updates your category each year based on the latest ITR and GST returns. Once you graduate to a higher category, you retain the benefits of the lower category for three years (graduation buffer) before the new limits apply. Downgrading happens too if investment and turnover drop in a sustained manner.

How do I print or download the certificate later?

add

You can log in to the Udyam portal any time with your URN and registered mobile, and download a PDF of the certificate with the live QR code. Buyers and banks scan the QR to verify your MSME status on the spot. There is no physical copy sent by post – the digital certificate is the authentic one.
